Yes Intune is rapidly getting there though.. The last few months they added a lot including encryption support. MS have a strategic alliance with JAMF for Macs but their end-game is to support them completely in Intune.

But their app deployment is still way too basic. You can only deploy apps in the /Applications folder, so nothing that installs device drivers etc.

Integrating JAMF with Intune adds reporting within a single interface, and the ability of conditional access to the JAMF-managed devices. We looked at it, but we decided to keep our Macs on WS1 until Intune is fully ready, instead of going to JAMF for a short time and then move to Intune proper when they're ready.
