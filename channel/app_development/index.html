Fed up with the general lack of version control of WebView Facebook recently swapped to an in house custom built WebView built on Chromium that they compile directly into their app. I’ve had similar frustrations with enterprise app reliance on WebView when compared with the limited controls we as EMM admins have over this relatively uncontrollable but critical system components. I have environments where the WebView is too old as devices aren’t automatically updating due to not hitting the right update criteria and other environments where brand new versions of WebView break things after devices do auto-update. In addition to that there is the general lack of version standardization across a fleet of devices as the updates slowly roll out and require the stars to align based on the Google Play requirements for app updates. With that being said, has anyone else considered migrating to custom WebView within your own app architecture to take back control like Facebook has in this instance? https://engineering.fb.com/2022/09/30/android/launching-a-new-chromium-based-webview-for-android/
